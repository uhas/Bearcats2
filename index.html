About us

Running your league, club or association shouldn’t be a full-time job (you already have one of those). Easy to learn and user friendly, our online sports management technology helps thousands of baseball, softball, football, soccer, hockey, and all types of sports organizers automate their administrative work and manage their players. In other words, you’ll spend a lot less time on the boring stuff so you can spend more time on the fun stuff.

For sports organizers that are just looking for a website that is simple, easy-to-use, and built according to the league’s needs, our self-setup eteamz websites are the solution for you. With do-it-yourself tools, you can create a professional, affordable site for your organization in no time—no experience necessary. Choose from a variety of free templates, add logos and photos, post scores and standings, and link to online registration.
